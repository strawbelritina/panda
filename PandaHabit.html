Panda Habitat

Pandas thrive in the misty, mountainous bamboo forests of central China, specifically in Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu provinces. These areas are cool, damp, and filled with dense bamboo—ideal for their survival. However, their natural habitat is shrinking due to deforestation and human activities.

 
-Key Habitat Features:
-High-altitude bamboo forests (1,200 4,000 meters above sea level)
-Cool and humid climate
-Abundant bamboo, their primary food source

Conservationists are working tirelessly to protect and restore these habitats to ensure a future for wild pandas
